# Google Play Draft

## App Name
DateHeart

## Short Description
Concrete date ideas for couples.

## Full Description
DateHeart gives couples a fast way to pick something to do together. Tap the animated heart and get a concrete date idea with budget, duration, preparation notes and tags.

Use filters for category, budget and duration, save favorite ideas and keep a short history of recent pulls. DateHeart includes thousands of localized ideas and a focused interface built for quick planning.

## Tags
Dating, Lifestyle, Relationships, Planning

## Review Notes
DateHeart is a practical date idea app. No login is required. Native Android submission should use Google Play Billing for any digital unlock such as no-ads.

## Data Safety Draft
- Location: not collected by the app backend; browser locale/time zone may be used locally to estimate currency.
- Email: only entered voluntarily for purchase restore and sent to the payment backend.
- Payment info: processed by Stripe or native store billing, not stored by DateHeart.
- App activity: favorites/history are stored locally on device.
