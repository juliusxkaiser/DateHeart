# App Store Draft

## Name
DateHeart

## Subtitle
Date ideas for couples

## Promotional Text
Tap the heart and get a concrete date plan with preparation, budget and timing.

## Description
DateHeart helps couples decide what to do together without scrolling through vague lists. Tap the animated heart to get a specific date idea, then save favorites, check your history and filter by category, budget or duration.

DateHeart includes thousands of localized ideas, practical preparation notes and a fast one-tap flow designed for real couples making plans now.

## Keywords
date,dating,couples,relationship,ideas,planner,romance,activities

## Review Notes
DateHeart is a date idea generator for couples. No account is required for the core app. The optional web purchase uses Stripe; native iOS submission should use Apple In-App Purchase for digital unlocks.

## Age Rating Notes
No user-generated public content, no gambling, no explicit content. Some activities may involve going to public venues; users choose what is safe and appropriate.
