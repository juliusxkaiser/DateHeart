Data processed on this device

DateHeart stores favorites, history, filters, language and purchase status in local browser storage. This data stays on the device unless the user clears browser/app data.

Payments

Payments are handled by Stripe. Stripe processes payment details, billing details and fraud-prevention data under Stripe's own terms and privacy policy.

Purchase restore

When a user restores a purchase, DateHeart sends the entered checkout email to the payment backend to ask Stripe whether a paid DateHeart purchase exists.

Ads and analytics

The current app build does not load third-party ad or analytics SDKs. Reserved ad placements are present in the product code but disabled by default.
