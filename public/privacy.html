Data processed on this device

DateHeart stores favorites, history, filters, language, no-ads purchase status and DateHeart Pro status in local browser storage. This data stays on the device unless the user clears browser/app data.

Payments

Web payments for the one-time no-ads unlock and DateHeart Pro subscriptions are handled by Stripe. Native iOS and Android purchases are handled by Apple In-App Purchase or Google Play Billing. Payment providers process payment details, billing details, subscription status and fraud-prevention data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Purchase restore

When a web user restores a purchase, DateHeart sends the entered checkout email to the payment backend to ask Stripe whether a paid DateHeart purchase or active DateHeart Pro subscription exists. Native purchase restores use the app store purchase APIs.

Ads and analytics

The web version does not load third-party ad or analytics SDKs. Native iOS and Android builds can load Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform to show ads and manage consent choices.

AdMob may process IP address, device identifiers, approximate location derived from IP address, app/ad interactions and diagnostic data to deliver, measure, limit and protect ads. Users in supported regions can manage ad consent choices through the in-app ad privacy control.
