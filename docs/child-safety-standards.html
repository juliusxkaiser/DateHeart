Child Safety Standards – DateHeart

App: DateHeart
Developer: Julius Kaiser
Contact: ceo@juliuskaiser.app

Audience

DateHeart is a dating-idea app intended exclusively for adults aged 18 and older. Users below the age of 18 are not permitted to use the app.

Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

DateHeart has a zero-tolerance policy toward any content that sexually exploits or abuses minors. The app does not allow user-generated content. All idea content is editorial and curated. No content involving minors in a sexual context is permitted, created, or distributed through this app under any circumstances.

Reporting

Any concerns related to child safety can be reported to ceo@juliuskaiser.app. Reports are investigated promptly. Where legally required, reports are forwarded to the relevant national authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and equivalent regional bodies.

Compliance

DateHeart complies with all applicable laws regarding the protection of minors, including but not limited to COPPA (US), GDPR-K (EU), and equivalent national legislation. The app does not collect personal data from minors.
