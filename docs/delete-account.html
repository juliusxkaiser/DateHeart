Delete Your DateHeart Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your DateHeart account and all associated data by following the steps below.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including your profile, relationship memories, and subscription information. This cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the DateHeart app on your device
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm by entering your password
  6. Tap Delete My Account to confirm

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user profile and account credentials
  • All relationship data, memories, and notes
  • Partner connection and shared data
  • Push notification tokens

What Is Retained

  • Purchase receipts (required for financial/legal compliance, retained for up to 7 years)
  • Anonymized crash logs (no personal data, retained for 90 days)
